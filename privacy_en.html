PINK TAPE Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026.05.31

1. Information we collect

PINK TAPE does not offer sign-up or login, and does not collect directly identifying information such as your name, email, or phone number on any server. The videos you record and your editing data are stored only on your device.

2. Camera & microphone access

PINK TAPE requests camera and microphone access in order to record video and capture audio. Recorded video and audio are never uploaded to any app server; all processing happens on your device.

3. Photo library access

When you export (save) a finished tape, we request permission to save it to your photo library. We do not read or transmit other photos from your library for any purpose other than saving.

4. Information stored on your device

To provide the service, the app stores on your device the video clips you record, your editing data (captions, trims, effects), whether a subscription is active, and whether first-run guidance has been shown.

5. Sharing and exporting

When you tap export or share, your video may be passed along through the standard iOS share sheet. Any app or service you choose to share with is governed by that service's own privacy policy.

6. Payment information

Subscription payments are processed through the Apple App Store's payment system. The app does not directly collect or store payment details such as card numbers; it only stores whether a subscription is active, locally on your device.

7. Retention and deletion

Video and editing data stored on your device are deleted together with the app when you uninstall it. Deleting a tape inside the app removes it from your device immediately.

8. Third-party disclosure

PINK TAPE does not sell or provide your videos or personal information to third parties, and does not perform advertising-related tracking.
